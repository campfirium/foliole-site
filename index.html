Principles

Built around ownership and continuity

Local-first by default

Your workspace lives in your own SQLite file, designed around personal data ownership.

Not a flashcard clone

Foliole is positioned as an incremental reading workspace, not an Anki or SuperMemo replacement.

One continuous loop

Reading, extraction, item creation, and review stay close enough to reinforce each other.
